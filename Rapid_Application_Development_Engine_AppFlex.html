Rapid Application Development Engine (AppFlex)

Business process management attempts to improve processes continuously. It could therefore be described as a “Process optimization” process. It has been found that BPM enables companies to more efficient, more effective and more capable of adopting to change than a functionally focused, traditional hierarchical management approach. Business Process Management (BPM) solutions and services help companies to automate their business processes in very reasonable time. The end results are improved customer experience and higher productivity efficiencies and operational savings as well. Sequentec provides Business Process Management solution for small to large sized businesses. Our BPM solutions and services offering enable companies to design and deploy BPM workflow applications using tools with which they are already familiar with and Sequentec's proprietary software.

Our services offerings include

  • Consulting
  • Implementation

AppFlex Studio has been created with a thought highly influenced with the concept. It is a platform where the requirements take the shape of real-time working software within the succinct timelines and satisfactory results. It takes the raw input in terms of analytical understanding of the specifications and processes them to provide visual results within nominal period.

AppFlex Studio also provide an ability to carry out modifications seamlessly and bringing in new elements of functionality alleviating the coupling pain. It works on the metadata provided and then takes care of most, if not all, of the pertaining alterations.

Features

  • Define entities and attributes
  • Define key players (users)
  • Define Roles
  • Assign defined roles to created users
  • Define User Interface based on entities
  • Define Validations for UI
  • Define dashboards
  • Define Processes and stages
  • Reroute Process flow at runtime
  • Design UIs at runtime (control placement)

Benefits

  • Auto generated, light-weight UIs
  • Various skins
  • Simple process maintenance
  • Effortless skin switching
  • Database independence
